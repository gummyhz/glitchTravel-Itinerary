Taiwan / 台灣

Travel Itinerary

Yangmingshan National Park

陽明山國家公園

Yangmingshan National Park has many mountains, trees, and a lot of wildlife, as well as hot springs and this flower clock. It is between Taipei and New Taipei City.

Taipei 101

台北101

Taipei 101 is in Taipei. It is a skyscraper and was the world's talles building from 2004 to 2010. There are also fountains outside that come out of the ground, which is cool.

Window on China Theme Park

小人國

Window on China Theme Park is in Taoyuan City. It has many miniature models of real life places, with paths inbetween so people can walk around and look at them.

National Palace Museum

國立故宮博物院

National Palace Museum is in Taipei. It holds almost 700,00 pieces of ancient Chinese imperial artifacts, covering 8,000 years of art history.

Yushan National Park

玉山國家公園

Yushan National Park is named after its talled mountain, Yushan, and encompasses a lot of the Central Mountain Range.

Shilin Night Market

士林夜市

Shilin Night Market is in Taipei. It is one of the largest and more popular night markets in Taiwan.
